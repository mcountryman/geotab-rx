Interface IDVIRLog

A DVIRLog is a Driver Vehicle Inspection Report which is prepared by a driver regarding defects in parts of a vehicle associated with a IDevice or ITrailer. Once the report is completed with optional driver remarks, the DVIR log will be acted upon, and marked as repairs made or not necessary (usually by another IUser). The driver then will mark the log as certified for being safe or unsafe to operate based on the effectiveness of any repairs made or comments for repairs not performed.
