Interface IFuelTaxDetail

Fuel tax reporting element. The available driving history for a is stored as a sequence of such details. Each next detail starts when and where the previous detail ended. A detail is identified by its parameters (enter and exit time, odometer, GPS odometer, latitude and longitude) and its attributes (jurisdiction, , toll road, authority). When any of the attributes changes, the current detail ends and a new detail begins. For more information, see IFTA Guide.
