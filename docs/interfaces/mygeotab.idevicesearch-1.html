engineVehicleIdentificationNumber

engineVehicleIdentificationNumber: string
  • Defined in packages/mygeotab/src/models.ts:5428

Gets or sets search for a Device by Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) reported by the engine. This is the unique number assigned to the vehicle during manufacturing and reported through any non-legacy GO device. This differs from in that it is the last VIN reported from the Device, and will be updated whether it is classified as valid or invalid.
